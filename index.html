    What Is Dork Mugs?

    Dork Mugs is an independent mug shop built for people who take their coffee as seriously as their opinions. Every design is original, printed on premium 11oz ceramic that is dishwasher safe, microwave safe, and built to outlast your most questionable life decisions.

    We make mugs for scientists, skeptics, music lovers, and the kind of people who read the footnotes. If you've ever felt slightly out of place in a world that takes itself too seriously, you're in the right spot. Browse the shop, pick a mug that actually represents you, and stop settling for generic.

    BP Collection

    Bold, direct, and unapologetically weird. The BP Collection is for people who aren't trying to fit in. These mugs say what they mean and mean what they say. Start your morning with some conviction and let the mug do the talking before your first sip.

    STEM Collection

    For the biologists, chemists, physicists, and professional overthinkers. The STEM Collection celebrates science with a sense of humor, because your lab coat doesn't have to match your personality. Biology jokes, chemistry puns, and physics references that actually land.

    Fame & Infamy

    Dark, gothic, and a little dramatic. Drawn from music culture, street art, and the kind of aesthetic that makes your coworkers ask questions. The Fame & Infamy Collection is made for people who leave a mark and aren't sorry about it.

    Premium Quality, Made to Last

    Every Dork Mug is made-to-order on 11oz ceramic with high-quality dye-sublimation printing. The designs won't fade, crack, or peel — they're built to handle daily use, the dishwasher, and your Monday morning mood. No cheap transfers, no peeling graphics, just a solid mug with a design that holds up. New styles drop regularly across all three collections, so check back often or follow us on social media to catch new releases first.
