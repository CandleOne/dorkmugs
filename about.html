    About Dork Mugs

    Original designs for people who think too much and drink too much coffee.

    Who We Are

    Dork Mugs is a small independent shop making original ceramic mug designs for the bold, the nerdy, and the unapologetically themselves. We believe your morning mug should say something — ideally something your coworkers will have to Google.

    We're not a big-box store. Every design is created in-house and printed on premium 11oz ceramic mugs through our print-on-demand partnership with Printify. That means no warehouse full of unsold inventory, no mass production, no guessing — just your mug, made when you order it.

    Our Collections

    BP Collection

    Bold, provocative, and unapologetically you. Mugs for people who have strong opinions and aren't afraid to drink coffee next to them.

    STEM Collection

    Science-themed designs for the beautifully nerdy. Biology puns, chemistry jokes, and general academic menace energy, bottled in ceramic.

    Fame & Infamy

    Gothic elegance for those who leave a mark. Dark, dramatic designs with enough gravitas to intimidate your entire morning.

    Print On Demand

    Every mug is made fresh through Printify's global network of print providers. This lets us offer a wide range of original designs without the waste of traditional retail — and means your mug hasn't been gathering dust on a shelf. It's made for you, when you order it.
