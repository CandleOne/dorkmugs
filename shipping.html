    Shipping Information

    We ship via Printify's network of print providers. Here's what to expect.

      Free shipping on all orders over $40 — automatically applied at checkout.

    How It Works

    Every Dork Mugs order is print-on-demand. That means your mug is printed fresh after you order — it's not sitting in a warehouse. Once it rolls off the press, it ships directly to your door from the nearest Printify print provider.

    Processing Time

    After you place your order, allow 2–5 business days for production. This is the time it takes to print and quality-check your mug before it ships. Orders placed on weekends or holidays begin production the next business day.

    Delivery Estimates

    Region Estimated Delivery Carrier
    United States (Standard) 5–10 business days after production USPS, UPS, or FedEx
    United States (Express) 2–4 business days after production UPS or FedEx

    Total time from order to delivery is typically 7–15 business days for standard shipping within the US. Express options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

    Shipping Rates

    • Orders over $40: Free standard shipping
    • Orders under $40: Standard shipping rates calculated at checkout
    • Express shipping: Available at an additional cost, calculated at checkout

    Order Tracking

    You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number as soon as your order ships. Use the link in the email to follow your mug's journey to your door.

    Questions About Your Order?

    If your order is delayed, lost, or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately and we'll sort it out. We stand behind every mug we ship.
